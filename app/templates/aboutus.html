About Us

Who We Are

Strategic Analytix is a reputable and growing firm that focuses on mission critical services to the Federal Government including the Department of Defense, Department of Homeland Security, and various intelligence agencies and organizations. With a reputation for innovation and development, Strategic Analytic has become a trusted partner through adopting unique skills and experience in order to secure a strong track record of customer success.

Our talented team of industry experts provide full life cycle development and mission support with a focus in - cyber security, systems engineering, intelligence analysis, voice analytics, and program management and support. Strategic Analytix is a privately held small-business headquartered in Columbia, MD just outside of Fort Meade and the National Security Agency.

Our Company Mission

To become the leading innovator and provider in security and defense services.
