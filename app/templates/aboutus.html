About Us

Who We Are

Strategic Analytix is a reputable and growing firm that focuses on mission critical services to the Federal Government including the Department of Defense and the Intelligence community. With a reputation for innovation and development, Strategic Analytix is a trusted partner adopting unique skills and experiences. Strategic Analytix has a dedicated commitment to our clients to deliver unparralled services and work diligently to serve our customers' missions.

Our talented team of industry experts provide full life cycle development and mission support with a focus in areas such as cyber security, systems engineering, programmatic and acqusition support. Our specialists hold numerous credentials including Top Secret/SCI security clearances, post graduate degrees, PMP, ITIL, CSM, CPA, and additional certifications to provide value added solutions. Strategic Analytix is a privately held minority owned, small-business headquarted in Fulton, MD just outside of Fort Meade and the National Security Agency.
